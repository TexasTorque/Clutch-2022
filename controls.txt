Driver
======

Left X Axis: drivebase translation (y)
Left Y Axis: drivebase translation (x)
Right X Axis: drivebase rotation

Right Bumper: increment speed setting
Left Bumper: decrement speed setting

Right Trigger: intake + mag
Left Trigger: detection shoot sequence

A Button: winch out (temporary)
B Button: winch in (temporary)
X Button: auto climb
Y Button: toggle servos (temporary)

DPAD Up: climber arms up
DPAD Down: climber arms down
DPAD Right: right climber arm down slowly
DPAD Left: left climber arm down slowly

Right Center: reset climber
Left Center:
